Get your add-on statistics from Devot:ee

How it works

NSM Devot:ee Stats gets your add-on's details from Devot:ee to use on your ExpressionEngine website.

Features

  • Get your ratings, reviews, favourites count and more for your add-on
  • Caches data in your ExpressionEngine cache directory
  • Provides the tag variables needed to display your Devot:ee stats
